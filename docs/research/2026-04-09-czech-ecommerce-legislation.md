## Key Findings

### 1. Obchodni podminky (Terms & Conditions)

**Governing laws:** Zakon c. 89/2012 Sb., obcansky zakonik (SS 1751-1753, SS 1810-1820); Zakon c. 634/1992 Sb., o ochrane spotrebitele.

Mandatory content:
- Seller identification (obchodni firma/jmeno, ICO, DIC, sidlo, zapisy v OR)
- Product/service description and key characteristics
- Total price including DPH and all fees, delivery costs
- Payment methods, delivery methods and deadlines
- Complaint procedure (reklamacni rad)
- Right of withdrawal: conditions, 14-day period, process, withdrawal form template
- Exceptions where withdrawal is not possible (SS 1837)
- Information about ADR/mimosoudni reseni sporu
- Personal data processing information
- Contract duration, termination conditions

**Key rule:** Terms cannot contain clauses creating "znacnou nerovnovahu" (significant imbalance) to the detriment of the consumer -- such clauses are invalid even if the consumer accepted them (SS 1813-1815). Terms must be published on the website, buyer must agree during purchase, and seller must send them via email with order confirmation.

### 2. Odstoupeni od smlouvy (Right of Withdrawal)

**Governing law:** Obcansky zakonik SS 1829-1837.

- **14-day period** from goods receipt (SS 1829). For services: 14 days from contract conclusion. For multiple-item orders: from receipt of last item.
- **No reason required.** Consumer can use any form (email is sufficient), but a withdrawal form template must be provided.
- **Refund deadline:** Seller must refund ALL payments including original delivery cost within 14 days of withdrawal (SS 1832). Same payment method unless consumer agrees otherwise.
- **Seller may withhold refund** until goods are returned or return shipment is proven (SS 1832 odst. 4).
- **Return shipping costs:** Consumer bears them, UNLESS seller failed to inform about this obligation per SS 1820(1)(j).
- **If seller fails to inform about withdrawal right:** Period extends to 1 year + 14 days (SS 1829 odst. 4).

**Exceptions (SS 1837)** -- consumer CANNOT withdraw from:
- Custom/bespoke goods (d)
- Perishable goods (e)
- Sealed hygiene goods once opened (g)
- Sealed audio/video/software once opened (h)
- Digital content after performance began with consent (l)
- Services fully performed before withdrawal period expired, with prior consent (a)
- Goods with price-dependent on financial market fluctuations (b)
- Newspapers, periodicals (i)
- Accommodation, transport, catering, leisure on specific dates (j)

**NEW (June 19, 2026):** Easy withdrawal mechanism -- "if a contract can be concluded with one click, withdrawal must be equally simple." E-shops must add a one-click withdrawal button.

### 3. Reklamace (Warranty / Prava z vadneho plneni)

**Governing law:** Obcansky zakonik SS 2099-2117, SS 2161-2174; Zakon 634/1992 Sb. SS 13, SS 19.

- **24-month period:** Consumer can claim defects appearing within 24 months from receipt (SS 2165).
- **Burden of proof (first 12 months):** Defect appearing within 12 months is PRESUMED to have existed at handover (SS 2161 odst. 2). Seller must prove otherwise to reject the claim. (Changed from 6 months to 12 months by novela 374/2022 Sb., effective 6.1.2023.)
- **Processing deadline:** Seller must resolve the claim within **30 days** (SS 19 zakon 634/1992). Must decide within 3 business days whether to accept.
- **Missed deadline:** If not resolved in 30 days, consumer can withdraw from the contract.
- **Consumer remedies (SS 2169):** Repair, replacement with new item, proportionate discount, or contract termination (refund).
- **Free of charge:** Reklamace must be at no cost to the consumer.
- **Reklamacni rad** must be published on website with clear scope, conditions, and seller contact.

**NEW (2026 amendment):** If consumer chooses REPAIR, warranty extends from 24 months to **36 months** (3 years).

### 4. GDPR & Personal Data in E-commerce

**Governing laws:** GDPR (EU 2016/679); Zakon c. 110/2019 Sb. (ZZOOU); Zakon c. 480/2004 Sb. (obchodni sdeleni); Zakon c. 127/2005 Sb. (elektronicke komunikace, cookies).

**Privacy policy must contain:**
- Data controller identity and contact
- What data is collected and why (purposes)
- Legal basis for each processing activity
- Data retention periods
- Recipients / third parties
- Consumer rights (access, correction, deletion, portability, objection)
- Right to file complaint with UOOU

**Legal bases for e-shop processing:**
- **Contract performance** (cl. 6(1)(b) GDPR): name, address, email, phone for order fulfillment
- **Legitimate interest** (cl. 6(1)(f)): fraud prevention, basic analytics
- **Consent** (cl. 6(1)(a)): marketing emails, newsletters, remarketing cookies

**Marketing emails (zakon 480/2004 Sb.):**
- **Opt-in required** for non-customers
- **Soft opt-out** exception for existing customers (can send about similar products without prior consent, but must allow easy unsubscribe)
- Every message must be clearly marked as commercial, identify sender, include unsubscribe mechanism
- Penalty: up to **10,000,000 CZK** (UOOU enforces)

### 5. Cookies

**Governing law:** Zakon c. 127/2005 Sb. o elektronickych komunikacich, SS 89 odst. 3 (amended by 374/2021 Sb., effective 1.1.2022); GDPR for personal data aspect.

**Opt-in regime since January 1, 2022:**
- **Technical/essential cookies:** No consent needed
- **Analytical, preference, marketing cookies:** Prior explicit consent required (opt-in)
- Pre-checked boxes are INVALID
- "Reject" button must be equally prominent as "Accept"
- Closing banner without choosing does NOT equal consent
- Cookie walls (blocking access before consent) are PROHIBITED
- Consent validity: ~12 months recommended
- Re-ask after rejection: minimum 6 months

### 6. Informacni povinnosti (Information Obligations)

**Governing laws:** Obcansky zakonik SS 435, SS 1811, SS 1820; Zakon 634/1992 SS 9-13; Zakon 455/1991 Sb. (zivnostensky zakon).

**Must be displayed on website:**
- Business name / name and surname (OSVC)
- ICO (identification number)
- DIC (tax ID, if VAT payer)
- Sidlo (registered office address)
- Registration: "zapsana v obchodnim rejstriku vedenem u [court], oddil [X], vlozka [Y]"
- Contact: email, phone, postal address
- Supervisory authority: "Ceska obchodni inspekce" with address

**Order button:** Must clearly state payment obligation -- e.g., "Objednat a zaplatit" or "Objednavka zavazujici k platbe" (effective since 2023).

### 7. Ceny a DPH

**Governing laws:** Zakon c. 235/2004 Sb., o DPH; Zakon 634/1992 SS 12, 12a.

**Current VAT rates (since 1.1.2024):**
- **21%** zakladni sazba (standard rate)
- **12%** snizena sazba (reduced rate -- food, water, medicines, books)

**Omnibus directive (effective since 6.1.2023):**
- When advertising a discount, seller MUST show the lowest price from the preceding **30 days** (SS 12a zakon 634/1992)
- Fines: Up to 5,000,000 CZK or 4% of annual turnover

### 8. EET (Elektronicka evidence trzeb)

**Current status:** EET was **abolished** as of January 1, 2023.
**EET 2.0:** Planned effective **January 1, 2027** (draft law submitted February 2026).

### 9. Zakon o ochrane spotrebitele (634/1992 Sb.)

Key sections: SS 9-11 (product info), SS 12/12a (prices/Omnibus), SS 13 (reklamace info), SS 14 (ADR info), SS 18a (marketplace transparency), SS 19 (30-day complaint deadline), SS 24-24a (fines up to 5M CZK or 4% turnover).

### 10. DSA / DMA

**DSA (EU 2022/2065):** Directly applicable since Feb 17, 2024. Czech coordinator: CTU. Czech adaptation law ("zakon o digitalni ekonomice") NOT yet adopted (expected 2026).

**DMA:** EU-level enforcement, applies to gatekeepers, not directly to Czech e-shops.

### 11. Zakon o pristupnosti (European Accessibility Act)

**Zakon c. 424/2023 Sb.** Effective **June 28, 2025**.
- WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance required
- **June 28, 2025:** New digital products/services must comply
- **June 28, 2030:** All existing products must comply
- **Exemption:** Micro-enterprises (<10 employees AND <EUR 2M turnover)
- Enforcement: COI

### 12. ADR/ODR

- E-shop must inform about ADR subject: "Ceska obchodni inspekce, Ustredni inspektorat -- oddeleni ADR, Stepanska 567/15, 120 00 Praha 2, https://adr.coi.cz"
- **EU ODR platform closed July 20, 2025** -- remove ODR link
- Penalty for non-compliance: up to 1,000,000 CZK

### 13. Smlouva uzavrena distancnim zpusobem

**Obcansky zakonik SS 1820-1840:**
- SS 1820: 19 specific pre-contractual information items
- SS 1824: Confirmation on durable medium after contract conclusion
- SS 1825: Review step before final confirmation, error correction
- SS 1826: Contract text archived and accessible to consumer

### 14. Shipping, Delivery, Risk Transfer

- Risk passes to consumer ONLY upon **physical receipt** (not handover to carrier)
- Seller bears transport damage risk until consumer receives goods
- Default delivery deadline: 30 days if not specified

### 15. Elektronicke podpisy

- E-shop purchase contracts do NOT require electronic signatures
- Purchase process (order button + email confirmation) creates valid contract
- Email valid for withdrawal, complaints, contract communications

## Actionable Insights — Compliance Checklist

### Website Must Display:
- [ ] Business name, ICO, DIC (if applicable), sidlo
- [ ] Registration details (obchodni rejstrik / zivnostensky rejstrik)
- [ ] Contact: email, phone, postal address
- [ ] All prices in CZK including DPH
- [ ] Shipping costs clearly before checkout
- [ ] Order button text: "Objednat a zaplatit" or equivalent

### Legal Documents Required:
- [ ] Obchodni podminky (all 19 items per SS 1820)
- [ ] Reklamacni rad (complaint procedure)
- [ ] Withdrawal form template (vzorovy formular pro odstoupeni)
- [ ] Privacy policy (zasady ochrany osobnich udaju)
- [ ] Cookie policy with compliant consent banner

### Cookie Banner:
- [ ] Block non-essential cookies before consent
- [ ] Equally prominent Accept/Reject buttons
- [ ] No pre-checked boxes
- [ ] No cookie walls
- [ ] Granular control (analytics vs marketing)

### Consumer Rights:
- [ ] 14-day withdrawal (no reason)
- [ ] Refund within 14 days of withdrawal
- [ ] 24-month complaint period
- [ ] 30-day complaint resolution deadline
- [ ] Free complaint processing
- [ ] ADR info with COI contact

### Discount/Price Rules:
- [ ] Show lowest price from last 30 days when advertising discount
- [ ] Maintain price evidence records

### Accessibility (from June 28, 2025, unless micro-enterprise):
- [ ] WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
- [ ] Accessibility statement published

### Marketing Emails:
- [ ] Opt-in consent for non-customers
- [ ] Easy unsubscribe in every message
- [ ] Marked as commercial communication
