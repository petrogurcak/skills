# Negotiation Plugin — Book Acquisition List

**13 books + 1 free paper.** Prioritized by leverage per book. Each entry: exact title, author, year, edition note, ISBN (10 + 13 where helpful), CZ translation status, sub-skill mapping, why canonical.

## TIER 1 — Must-have (5 books)

These 5 are non-negotiable. Every reference file in the plugin draws from at least one.

### 1. Chris Voss & Tahl Raz — _Never Split the Difference_

- **Year:** 2016
- **Edition:** First edition (only edition; no revisions)
- **ISBN-13:** 9780062407801
- **Publisher:** Harper Business
- **Pages:** 288
- **CZ translation:** _Nikdy nedělej kompromis_ (Jan Melvil Publishing, 2016, transl. Petr Hanák)
- **CZ ISBN:** 9788075550583
- **Sub-skill:** `tactical-empathy` (Tier 1 canonical)
- **Why canonical:** The only modern verbal-tactics book that operationalizes FBI hostage-negotiation primitives — mirroring, labeling, calibrated questions, late-night-FM-DJ voice, no-oriented questions, Black Swans, Ackerman bargaining. No substitute exists. **Recommend EN PDF — Voss's voice + structure are clearer in original.**

### 2. Joe Navarro & Marvin Karlins — _What Every Body Is Saying: An Ex-FBI Agent's Guide to Speed-Reading People_

- **Year:** 2008
- **Edition:** First edition
- **ISBN-13:** 9780061438295
- **Publisher:** William Morrow Paperbacks
- **Pages:** 250
- **CZ translation:** _Tajemství řeči těla_ (Grada, 2nd CZ ed. 2019). Older CZ ed. _Jak poznat, když vám někdo lže_ (2013).
- **CZ ISBN (Grada 2019):** 9788027119189
- **Sub-skill:** `reading-people` (Tier 1 canonical)
- **Why canonical:** Foundational field manual. Limbic freeze→flight→fight, pacifying behaviors, feet→legs→torso→arms→hands→face honesty hierarchy, Ten Commandments of observation. Every nonverbal-skill author treats this as baseline.

### 3. Robert Cialdini — _Influence: The Psychology of Persuasion_

- **Year:** 2021 (New & Expanded edition — **NOT** the 1984 original)
- **Edition:** New & Expanded (adds 7th principle Unity + 2010s research updates)
- **ISBN-13:** 9780062937650
- **Publisher:** Harper Business
- **Pages:** 592
- **CZ translation:** _Nové zbraně vlivu_ (2023, current ed. matching 2021 expansion). Older _Zbraně vlivu_ (multiple eds.) is the 1984 original — **don't confuse these**.
- **Sub-skill:** Used by `tactical-empathy`, `batna-strategy`, `written-negotiation` via shared reference `cialdini-7-principles.md`
- **Why canonical:** 7 principles (reciprocity, commitment/consistency, social proof, authority, liking, scarcity, unity). The bias layer of every negotiation. **Get the 2021 version specifically** — older eds. lack Unity + 2010s replication updates.

### 4. Roger Fisher, William Ury & Bruce Patton — _Getting to Yes: Negotiating Agreement Without Giving In_

- **Year:** 2011 (3rd edition)
- **Edition:** **3rd edition** (revised, with Patton co-author). 1st ed. 1981, 2nd ed. 1991.
- **ISBN-13:** 9780143118756
- **Publisher:** Penguin Books
- **Pages:** 240
- **CZ translation:** _Jak dosáhnout souhlasu: Zásady úspěšného vyjednávání_ (Management Press). Older CZ ed. _Dohoda jistá_ (1994).
- **Sub-skill:** `batna-strategy` (Tier 1 canonical)
- **Why canonical:** Founding text of principled negotiation. 4 pillars (separate people / interests not positions / options for mutual gain / objective criteria) + BATNA. Non-negotiable shared vocabulary. **Get 3rd ed. specifically** — adds Patton's clarifications, modern examples.

### 5. Radim Pařík — _Umění vyjednat cokoliv_

- **Year:** 2024 (updated 2025 ed.)
- **Edition:** Latest available (verify 2025 update at grada.cz)
- **Publisher:** Grada
- **CZ ONLY** — no English translation. Pařík completed FBI negotiation program + Harvard. Founded Asociace vyjednavačů.
- **Sub-skill:** `negotiation` orchestrator + `cz-business-culture-deltas.md` reference (CZ Tier 1)
- **Why canonical:** The single most-important CZ-language synthesis. Adapts Voss/Navarro to Czech business reality with examples Petr can directly recognize. **The CZ-language anchor for the entire plugin.**

## TIER 2 — Strongly recommended (4 books)

These 4 fill gaps Tier 1 doesn't cover.

### 6. Mark Goulston — _Just Listen: Discover the Secret to Getting Through to Absolutely Anyone_

- **Year:** 2009 (1st ed., still current)
- **ISBN-13:** 9780814436479
- **Publisher:** AMACOM
- **Pages:** 240
- **CZ translation:** None found
- **Sub-skill:** `emotional-conflict` (Tier 2) + `goulston-9-rules.md` reference
- **Why canonical:** Psychiatrist's take on tactical empathy in high-emotion contexts. Persuasion Cycle (Resisting → Listening → Considering → Willing → Doing → Glad I did) + 9 rules + empathy jolt. Stronger than Voss for cofounder/firing/family scenarios where "tactical" reads cold.

### 7. Deepak Malhotra & Max Bazerman — _Negotiation Genius: How to Overcome Obstacles and Achieve Brilliant Results at the Bargaining Table and Beyond_

- **Year:** 2007 (1st ed., still current)
- **ISBN-13:** 9780553384116
- **Publisher:** Bantam
- **Pages:** 343
- **CZ translation:** None found
- **Sub-skill:** `batna-strategy` (Tier 1, depth)
- **Why canonical:** Harvard Business School complementary masterpiece to _Getting to Yes_. Where Fisher/Ury is principles, this is _plays_: ZOPA framing, anchor-shifting, multi-issue trades, bias exploitation/defense, contingency contracts, defenses against manipulation tactics (Ch. 10-11).

### 8. Joe Navarro — _Be Exceptional: Master the Five Traits That Set Extraordinary People Apart_

- **Year:** 2021
- **ISBN-13:** 9780063093430
- **Publisher:** William Morrow
- **Pages:** 256
- **CZ translation:** _Buďte výjimeční_ (Grada, 2022)
- **CZ ISBN (Grada):** 9788027141425
- **Sub-skill:** `reading-people` (Tier 2) — leadership/influence frame
- **Why canonical:** Synthesis frame. 5 domains (Self-Mastery, Observation, Communication, Action, **Psychological Comfort**). Psychological Comfort is the master ethical frame for the entire plugin — "make people feel safe enough to be honest with you" is the orientation post-2018.

### 9. Douglas Stone, Bruce Patton & Sheila Heen — _Difficult Conversations: How to Discuss What Matters Most_

- **Year:** 2010 (10th Anniversary edition — **NOT** the 1999 original)
- **Edition:** 10th anniversary, with new preface by authors
- **ISBN-13:** 9780143118442
- **Publisher:** Penguin Books
- **Pages:** 352
- **CZ translation:** _Obtížné rozhovory_ (verify availability — referenced in databazeknih, patchy stock)
- **Sub-skill:** `emotional-conflict` (Tier 2) + `difficult-conversations-three-frame.md` reference
- **Why canonical:** Harvard Negotiation Project lineage. Three-conversations frame (What Happened / Feelings / Identity). The cleanest tool for cofounder split / firing / relationship contexts. **Get 10th anniversary** — author preface contextualizes 21st-century applications.

## TIER 3 — Nice to have (3 books)

These deepen specific skills but aren't blockers for v1.

### 10. Robert Cialdini — _Pre-Suasion: A Revolutionary Way to Influence and Persuade_

- **Year:** 2016
- **ISBN-13:** 9781501109799
- **Publisher:** Simon & Schuster
- **Pages:** 432
- **CZ translation:** _Před-svědčování_ (Jan Melvil Publishing)
- **Sub-skill:** `negotiation` orchestrator (Tier 2) — pre-meeting phase
- **Why canonical:** Sequel to _Influence_. Privileged moments + channeled attention frame — what's salient in attention right before the ask. Maps directly onto pre-meeting phase (room choice, opening question, opening anchor).

### 11. George Kohlrieser — _Hostage at the Table: How Leaders Can Overcome Conflict, Influence Others, and Raise Performance_

- **Year:** 2024 (2nd edition — **NOT** the 2006 original)
- **Edition:** 2nd edition (self-published, refreshed)
- **ISBN-13 (2nd ed.):** 9798228509726
- **Publisher (2nd ed.):** Self-published / IMD Lausanne distribution
- **CZ translation:** None found
- **Sub-skill:** `emotional-conflict` (Tier 2) — self-regulation prerequisite
- **Why canonical:** IMD Lausanne professor + practicing hostage negotiator. Translates hostage tactics into business/leadership. 7 keys to dialogue (bonding, mind's eye, loss & grief, secure base, empathic listening, persuasion via dialogue, focus on positive). The "self-regulation before tactics" prerequisite to anything Voss-style.

### 12. Adam Dolník & Martin Moravec — _Adam Dolník: Svět elitního vyjednavače_

- **Year:** 2024
- **CZ ONLY** — no English translation (Dolník trains FBI/Scotland Yard but the book is CZ-original)
- **Publisher:** Czech publisher (verify on databazeknih.cz #543931)
- **Sub-skill:** `tactical-empathy` (Tier 3) / `emotional-conflict` (CZ crisis examples)
- **Why canonical:** Czech equivalent to Noesner _Stalling for Time_. Czech who has actually negotiated with hostage-takers, kidnappers, Somali pirates, ISIS. **The single CZ-language source where extreme-stakes verbal tactics are described in Czech idiom.** Pure example fuel for skill content.

### 14. Vecchi, Van Hasselt & Romano — _Crisis (hostage) negotiation: Current strategies and issues in high-risk conflict resolution_

- **Year:** 2005
- **Journal:** _Aggression and Violent Behavior_ 10(5)
- **Free PDF:** https://www.joindpp.org/wp-content/uploads/crisis_hostage_negotiation_current_strat.pdf
- **Pages:** ~18
- **Sub-skill:** `tactical-empathy`, `emotional-conflict` via `bcsm-stairway.md` reference
- **Why canonical:** Academic source for Behavioral Change Stairway Model (BCSM): Active Listening → Empathy → Rapport → Influence → Behavioral Change. **Voss's book is essentially BCSM dressed in narrative**; this paper grounds the methodology in peer-reviewed framework. **Acquire immediately — free, takes 30 min to read.**

## What's NOT on this list (deliberately excluded)

These appeared in the broader corpus matrix but don't justify a slot in the must-have/strongly-recommended layers:

- **Allan Pease — Definitive Book of Body Language** — overlap with Navarro, less rigor
- **Vanessa Van Edwards — Cues** — modern repackaging, not primary
- **Robert Greene — Laws of Human Nature** — pop history, not peer-reviewed
- **Oren Klaff — Pitch Anything** — pitching-specific, narrow scope
- **Jeb Blount — INKED** — sales-side closing, narrow scope
- **Neil Rackham — SPIN Selling** — sales-only methodology
- **William Ury — Getting to Yes with Yourself** — useful only for self-clarity scenarios
- **Lax/Sebenius — 3-D Negotiation** — multi-party scope (out of v1 plugin scope)
- **Malhotra — Negotiating the Impossible** — deadlock-only specialization
- **Navarro — Three Minutes to Doomsday** — narrative memoir, not how-to (great if you want to _see_ methodology in action; skip if acquiring for plugin content)
- **Navarro — Dangerous Personalities** — narcissist/psychopath taxonomy; useful only for due-diligence scenarios. Acquire if/when due-diligence sub-skill is added (out of v1)
- **Navarro — Louder Than Words** — subsumed by _Be Exceptional_
- **Gary Noesner — Stalling for Time** — origin story of FBI doctrine (Voss's mentor's memoir). Skip; Voss's book covers operational kernel.
- **Paul Ekman — Telling Lies / FACS** — Navarro himself disowns the deception-detection framing post-2018. Don't include.
- **Daniel Kahneman — Thinking, Fast and Slow** — bias bedrock but Cialdini does negotiation-specific translation
- **Erin Meyer — The Culture Map** — cross-cultural beyond CZ (out of v1 plugin scope)
- **Vít Prokůpek — Psychologie ovlivňování** — practical CZ cheat-sheet but largely subsumed by Cialdini + Pařík combination. Acquire only if Tier 1-3 still leave gaps.
