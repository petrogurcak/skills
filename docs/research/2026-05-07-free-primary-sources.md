# Free Primary-Source Surrogates for Negotiation Canon

**Methodology:** For every paid book in the corpus matrix, identify free or near-free content authored by the same primary writer (or canonical interview transcript) that covers the methodology. Verified via WebFetch on key URLs. Prefer transcripts > video > audio (skill needs textual source).

**Why this matters:** Total free content available across the EN corpus = roughly 50–80 hours of audio/video + 200+ articles + 4 free academic PDFs. Sufficient to draft 70–80% of the EN sub-skills (`reading-people`, `tactical-empathy`, `batna-strategy`, `negotiation` orchestrator) before owning a single book. The remaining 20–30% (book-only frameworks like Voss's full Ackerman bargaining table, Cialdini's Pre-Suasion experiments) require acquisition.

The CZ corpus is differently shaped: less free per author, but Pařík/Dolník/Prokůpek each maintain native-CZ free YouTube/podcast catalogs that ARE the primary deliverable for CZ business culture deltas (no translation tax).
