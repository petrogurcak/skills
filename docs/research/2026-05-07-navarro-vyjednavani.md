## Key Findings — Navarrova metodologie (12 namedconcepts)

### 1. Limbic-driven responses: freeze / flight / fight
**Concept:** Limbicky mozek (ne neokortex) ridi vsechny upremne reakce. Hierarchie evolucne: **freeze → flight → fight**, ne fight-or-flight.
**Apply:** Pri stresoru (cena, deadline, podminka) sleduj nejdriv freeze — nahla nehybnost, mely dech, "turtle effect" (ramena nahoru). Pak flight — torzo/nohy se otaceji od tebe, naklon vzad, eye-blocking. Az nakonec fight — nafouknuti hrudniku, sevreni celisti, ukazovani prstem.
**Mistake:** Cekat na viditelnou agresi. Vetsina jednanovaho distresu je freeze (2-sekund pause, ztuhly ruka) — pozorovatele to mine.

### 2. Pacifying behaviors (uklidnujici chovani pod stresem)
**Concept:** Po limbickem stresu se mozek auto-uklidnuje self-touch → uvolneni endorfinu. Catalogue (Navarro):
- **Suprasternal notch** (jamka mezi klicni kostmi) — touch nejcastejsi u zen, **silnejsi tell**
- **Krk** — rubbing, kravata adjustment, ohrnovani limce
- **Leg cleanser** — ruce sliding po stehnech pod stolem (FBI watches: "almost always when confronted with damning evidence")
- **Ventilator** — odtahovani limce od krku
- Lip-licking, hair-tossing, forehead rubbing, self-hugging, cheek puff

**Apply:** Kdyz se objevi pacifier, mentalne bookmark **co bylo prave receno**. Rewind: ktera klauzule, cislo, otazka spustila? Tam je real friction. Pri review smlouvy nech druhou stranu cist line-by-line a sleduj pacifiers per klauzule.
**Mistake:** Brat pacifier jako proof of lying. Dokazuje jen **discomfort** — pricina muze byt tema, teplota mistnosti, nesouvisejici starost. Confirm re-introduction trigger + watch repetition.

### 3. Comfort vs discomfort — master frame
**Concept:** Navarroova binary master frame (post-2018): zapomen "lze ten clovek?" Ptej se jen **"vidim comfort nebo discomfort?"**
- **Comfort** = ventral fronting (torzo otevreno k tobe), gravity-defying gesta (oboci nahoru, ramena lehka, brada nahoru), full-color rty, relaxed limbs, head tilt s expozici krku
- **Discomfort** = ventral denial (torzo otoceno pryc), lip compression / disappearing lips (krev odtejka), zachmureni glabella (mezi obocim), zuzene ocni stverbiny, sevreni celisti, restrained gesta

**Apply:** Bezi continuous comfort/discomfort delta during meeting. Comfort spike po tve nabidce = blizky deal. Discomfort spike on specific phrase = land tam follow-up question.
**Mistake:** Snazit se labelovat konkretni emoce ("jsou nastvani / vystraseni / podezrivavi"). Binary je dost a o moc presnejsi.

### 4. Baseline behavior
**Concept:** Etabluj baseline v prvnich **3-5 minutach** na neutralnich tematech (pocasi, doprava, kafe). Loguj: hand position, blink rate, voice tempo, kam smeruji nohy, jestli fidgetuje, eye-contact duration. **Jen odchylky od baseline nesou signal.**
**Apply:** Pre-business small talk neni zdvorilost — je to data collection. V CZ kontextu (Cesi dele se mascuji emoce) je baseline-setting **2x dulezitejsi**: budes cist deviace od low baseline, ne absolute amplitude.
**Mistake:** Porovnavat protistranu se sebou nebo s genericky "confident person" template. Nekteri lide fidgetuji vzdy — jejich klid je tell, ne fidget.

### 5. Feet > face — hierarchy upremnosti
**Concept:** "Feet are most honest part of the body." Navarro hierarchy (od nejupremnesi po nejmin): **feet → legs → torso → arms → hands → face**. Obličej se ucime kontrolovat od detstvi, nohy nikdo neuci.
**Apply:** Sed tam, kde vidis nohy (open table > desk). Sleduj:
- *Happy feet* — bouncing = excitement OR impatience (kontext rozhodne)
- *Foot direction* — nohy ukazuji kam chceme (vychod, dvere, oblibena osoba, my pokud zajem)
- *Leg-splay* — territorialni confidence
- *Foot-lock kolem nohou zidle* — discomfort/restraint
- *Foot-withdrawal* — stahovani nohou pod zidli = retreat

**Mistake:** Civet na obličej. Az to ukaze face, nohy to ukazaly o 2 sekundy driv — a byly upremnejsi.

### 6. Territorialni display
**Concept:** Dominantni vyjednavaci zabiraji prostor:
- Ruce roztazene podel zidle / okraje stolu
- **Arms akimbo** (ruce v bok)
- **Hooding** (ruce za hlavou, lokty siroke)
- Lokty roztazene na stul do prostoru protistrany
- Veci roztrousene po stole (papiry, telefon, pero, lahev) jako "territorialni markers"
- Vybrani sedu na cele stolu

**Apply (offensive):** Prijdi klidne, vezmi seat s vyhledem na dvere, polozi veci zamerne. Nestahuj se. "Whoever controls time, controls" — taky: kdo prijde prvni, kde sedi, kolik jich prijde.
**Apply (defensive — Navarro lawyer trick):** Pozadej o **nejmensi mistnost dostupnou**. Navarro takhle neutralizoval 6-pravnicky tym tym, ze museli poslat 2 lidi — without confrontation. **Environment design beats negotiation tactics.**
**Mistake:** Mirroring agressivnich territorial grabs. Lepsi neutralize via room/seating choice nez eskalovat.

### 7. Hand gestures
- **Steepling** (spicky prstu se dotykaji, peakovany strecha): nejvyssi-confidence gesto. Pouzivej sparingly when stating position.
- **Palm-up** (asking, openness, "please believe me"): submisivni — nezbytne v rapport, ale overuse = weakness/pleading.
- **Palm-down** (asserting, declaring): autoritativni. Pri statingu non-negotiables. Ne aggressive — declarative.
- **Thumb display** (palce viditelne ven z kapes/u saka): high status / confidence. Tucked thumbs / closed fists = fear, low confidence.
- **Hidden hands** (pod stolem, v kapsach, za zady): generates suspicion. Always keep hands visible.
- **Interlaced fingers / hand-wringing**: low confidence, anxiety.

**Apply:** Otevri palms-up na rapport (Cialdini liking + reciprocity), shift na steepling pri statingu cisel, palm-down pri closingu.
**Mistake:** Default palm-up everything. Reads jako supplicant. Kalibruj na fazi.

### 8. Eye behavior
**Concept:**
- **Pupil dilation** = pozitivni interest (involuntary — nejde fakovat)
- **Pupil constriction** = negativni perception
- **Eye-blocking** (slow blinks, ruka k oci, oboci rub) = "I don't want to see/hear this" — classic discomfort
- **Blink-rate increase** = stress
- **Squint** = doubt or scrutiny
- **Eyebrow flash** (split-second nahoru) = recognition / pozitivni greeting

**Apply:** Rekni svoji nabidku a sleduj zorničky, ne face. Otazka "Sedi vam to?" — squint nebo blink-flutter = nesedi.
**Mistake:** Verit "lhari neudrzuji eye contact." Vrij + meta-analyses ukazuji opak: prakticti lhari **over-maintain** eye contact. Gaze aversion alone = junk signal.

### 9. The deception myth — Navarrova nuanced view (post-2018)
**Concept:** Navarro publishoval **The End of Detecting Deception** (Psychology Today, July 2018; jnforensics.com). Explicitni position:
> "There is no single behavior indicative of deception... and even those who look for clusters of behaviors would also be wrong — there are no clusters of behaviors indicative of deception."

Navarrov mentor Mark Frank: *"Joe, there is no Pinocchio effect."* Co body language **muze** rict: psychologicky discomfort — stress, anxiety, concern, tension. Pricinu zjisti **otazkami**, ne body-language readings.

**Apply:** Reframe "lze on?" jako "co ho dela uncomfortable, a je ten signal actionable v tomto vyjednavani?" Pak ptej se calibrated question (Voss-style "Jak to mam udelat?") to surface pricinu.

**Mistake:** Cist body language hrat lie-detector. Even FBI/Secret Service tops out near 60% accuracy (DePaulo/Bond meta-analysis: ~54% baseline, ~chance).

### 10. Three-minute insight (z *Three Minutes to Doomsday*)
**Concept:** Navarro rozkrl Conrad/Ramsay spy case na hand-tremble v opening minutach routinniho interview. **Lesson neni metoda zvana "Three Minutes"** — je to ze **prvni minuty obsahuji highest-information density** protoze counterpart se na tebe jeste neadaptoval. Skutecna technika z knihy:
- Pre-choreograph kazdy meeting
- Control fyzicke environment
- Keep small-talk lubricant flowing
- **Don't take notes** ("if you're writing, you're not observing")
- **Doorjamb confrontation** — klicovy reveal saved pro moment odchodu kdyz guard down

**Apply:** Naplanuj prvnich 5 minut peclive. Use small-talk to baseline. Withhold nejtezsi otazku pro moment kdyz si mysli ze meeting konci.
**Mistake:** Front-loadovat hard ask kdyz jsou jeste locked into negotiation posture.

### 11. ⚠️ "L-FECC" framework — NEEXISTUJE
**Tato akronyme se v Navarrove published work nikde neobjevuje.** Closest documented frames:
- **Comfort/Discomfort paradigm** (jnforensics.com / *Dictionary of Body Language*) — Navarro's currently preferred frame, simpler
- **Four-Domain Model for Detecting Deception** (Navarro, 2003 academic paper, ResearchGate) — comfort/discomfort + emphasis + synchrony + perception management — richer, pro skilled practitioners

**Doporuceni pro skill:** Pouzit Comfort/Discomfort binary jako primary framework. Four-Domain Model jako advanced variant. NEzminovat L-FECC.

### 12. Be Exceptional — 5 domains (2021)
1. **Self-Mastery** — foundational; "to lead others, first lead yourself." Honest self-criticism, deferred gratification.
2. **Observation** — read needs, fears, intentions of others before they speak.
3. **Communication** — verbal + nonverbal harmony; persuade, motivate, inspire.
4. **Action** — build shared purpose, lead by example, follow through.
5. **Psychological Comfort** — **core of influence**: people kteri se citi psychologically safe with you tell you the truth, do business with you, follow you.

**Apply to negotiation:** Psychological Comfort je unifier. Pred kazdou taktikou: udelal jsem tuto osobu psychologically safe enough to be honest se mnou? Pokud ne, zadna technika nebude fungovat.

## Negotiation flow — phase by phase application

### Pre-meeting
- **Mistnost:** Vyber **nejmensi viable room** (Navarrov lawyer trick) NEBO room kde uvidis nohy pod stolem.
- **Time:** Prijd 10 min pred. Pick seat s vyhledem na dvere + zady ke zdi. Polz veci zamerne (notebook, voda, telefon) — light territorial frame, ne aggressive.
- **CZ context:** Dress conservative (dark suit, no bright colors), printed agenda — Cesi prefer pisemne/strukturovane.

### Opening (prvnich 5 min — baseline)
- **Handshake:** Pevny + direct eye contact (CZ norm — slaby handshake = slaby clovek; absent eye contact = read jako lhar/skryvajici).
- **Small talk 2-3 min:** Pocasi, cesta, kava, hokej/fotbal. **Avoid:** politika, historia (zvlast WWII/komunismus), platy. Use **vykani** dokud invited otherwise ("Tykejme si").
- **Behem small talku:** Silent log baseline — blink rate, hand position, foot direction, voice tempo, posture.

### Probing
- State topic, ptej se calibrated question (Voss): *"Jak to vypada z vasi strany?"*
- Watch **deviations from baseline** during their answer: pacifier (neck-touch), foot-rotation toward exit, lip compression, blink-rate jump, sudden stillness (freeze).
- Mark **exact phrase** ktera spustila shift. Re-test pozdeji paraphrase pro confirmation.
- **CZ context:** Verbalni *"to bude tezke"* / *"uvidime"* almost always = **NE**. Don't take it as maybe.

### Pushback / koncese reading
- Counterpart objects → look for **synchrony** between words and body. "Toto cislo nam funguje" + head-shake / lip-purse / torso-pull-back = words wrong, body right.
- **Jejich pacifier kdyz TY proposes** = tve cislo lands hard. **Stay silent** (Voss tactical silence). Pacifying often resolves into koncesi protoze discomfort je real a need to release verbally.
- **Steepling** on jejich strane = confident in position; nedavej tam push, redirect na jinou variable.

### Closing
- Confirm verbalne + watch for **ventral fronting** (torzo se otevre, naklon dopredu), gravity-defying behaviors (oboci nahoru, ramena uvolnena), full-color rty, head tilt. Tento cluster = genuine commitment.
- Pokud vidis **ventral denial** (torzo angled away), foot-pointing-to-door, lip-disappearance during "ano" — **fake yes**. Slow down. Ptej se: *"Co vas na tom jeste trapi?"*
- **Doorjamb** — most candid info often after handshake u dveri. Ptej se one more question on the way out.

## Komplementarni autori — kdy pouzit (briefly)

### Chris Voss — *Never Split the Difference* (2016) — verbal layer
Tactical empathy = articulate emoce protistrany **pred** reseni problemu. Core moves:
- **Mirroring** — opakuj jejich last 1-3 slova, inquisitive ton
- **Labeling** — "Sounds like…", "Vypada to ze…" — verbalizace emoce deactivates amygdala
- **No-oriented questions** — "Je ted nevhodna doba?" lands lepe nez "Mate minutu?"
- **Late-night-FM-DJ voice** — slow, low, calm — induces trust
- **Calibrated questions** — "Jak to mam udelat?"
- **Black Swans** — hidden info ktera meni vsechno; surface jen kdyz counterpart feels safe

**Kombinace s Navarrem:** Voss = verbal layer of Navarro reading. Navarro cte body, Voss feeds words. Use Voss otazky aby surface pricinu Navarro detected discomfort.

### Fisher / Ury — *Getting to Yes* (1981) — content struktura
4 pillars:
1. **Separate people from problem**
2. **Focus on interests, not positions**
3. **Invent options for mutual gain**
4. **Use objective criteria**

**BATNA** = Best Alternative To a Negotiated Agreement. Strana s lepsi BATNA ma power. Vzdy znej svou; estimate jejich.

**Kombinace s Navarrem:** Navarrova metoda pomaha **odhalit jejich BATNA strength** — pokud jsou comfort+confident pri tve nabidce, jejich BATNA je silna. Pokud discomfort+pacifying, slabsi nez tvrdi.

### Cialdini — *Influence* (1984, updated 2021) — psychology bias
6 (now 7) principu:
- **Reciprocita** — give first, unexpected, personalized
- **Commitment/consistency** — small public yes leads to big yes
- **Social proof** — others like them already chose this
- **Authority** — credentials, track record, framing as expert
- **Liking** — similarity, compliments, cooperation
- **Scarcity** — limited time/quantity raises perceived value
- **Unity** (7th) — shared identity

**Kombinace s Navarrem:** Cialdini biases psychology, Navarro confirms it landed. Po reciprocity move sleduj jejich body — comfort spike potvrzuje effect, neutral baseline = nelandlo.

## Vedecke kritiky — fer assessment

### Well-supported (use freely)
- Limbic-driven autonomic responses (pupil dilation, blush, sweating, blink-rate changes pod stresem) — robust neuroscience
- Comfort/discomfort jako binary observable indicator psychologickeho state — well-supported
- Synchrony / mirroring increases liking + rapport — replicated
- Baseline-deviation detection > absolute-cue judgment

### Debated / Weakly-supported (caveat in skill)
- **Microexpressions jako deception detector** — Ekman/O'Sullivan 1991 work criticized for selective methodology. Porter & ten Brinke: jen ~2% high-stakes expressions byly microexpressions. Jordan et al. (2019): Ekman METT training NEzlepsil accuracy beyond chance. Frontiers in Psychology 2018 (PMC6158306): "Microexpressions Are Not the Best Way to Catch a Liar" — frequency too low, controllability higher than claimed, accuracy below 56%.
- **Body-language-as-lie-detection** — Vrij meta-analyses: ~54% accuracy laici i profesionalove (chance = 50%). Vrij central finding: pozornost na nonverbal cues **REDUCES** accuracy vs listening to verbal content. Bond & DePaulo (2006) meta-analysis 200+ studies: zadna profession reliable above chance.
- **Single tells** (gaze aversion, fidgeting, neck-touch) — individually nereliable. Practiced lhari often **suppress** these + **over-maintain** eye contact.

### Navarrov own current position (2018+)
Navarro **se shoduje s critics on deception**: zadny Pinocchio effect, zadny reliable deception cluster. Explicitne: cti pro **psychological discomfort**, ne **deception**. Pak verbalne investigate cause discomfortu. Aligns s Vrij "cognitive approach" — increase cognitive load via questioning, then watch verbal content inconsistencies, ne body cues.

### Practical implication for skill
**Frame the skill kolem reading discomfort to find leverage points and unresolved concerns** — ne kolem catching lhari. Tato position je:
- empirically defensible
- ethically clean
- matches Navarro's most recent published view
