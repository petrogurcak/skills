## Sub-skill 1: `reading-people` (Navarro nonverbal core)

### Tier 1 — Required canon

**Joe Navarro — *What Every Body Is Saying* (2008)**
- The single foundational field manual. Limbic freeze→flight→fight, pacifying behaviors, feet→legs→torso→arms→hands→face honesty hierarchy.
- **CZ — *Tajemství řeči těla* (Grada, 2019, 2nd CZ ed.)**, plus older *Jak poznat, když vám někdo lže* (2013).
- EN audiobook: Storytel/Audible (Paul Costanzo). **No CZ audiobook of this exact ed.** Alternative: *Jak prokouknout druhé lidi* (Audiolibrix 7h24min, 500 Kč, narr. Zbořil, 4.53/5) — older translation but same core content.

**Joe Navarro — *The Dictionary of Body Language* (2018)**
- 400+ catalogued behaviors, head-to-toe. Reference manual. Embeds Navarro's post-2018 comfort/discomfort frame.
- EN: hardcover + Storytel audiobook. **No CZ translation** as of 2026-05.

### Tier 2 — Strong supplements

**Joe Navarro — *Be Exceptional* (2021) / CZ *Buďte výjimeční* (Grada 2022)**
- Synthesis frame. 5 domains (Self-Mastery, Observation, Communication, Action, Psychological Comfort) — the ethical pivot the skill needs.
- **CZ hardcover only** (Grada 2022). EN audiobook on Audible/Storytel.

**Allan & Barbara Pease — *The Definitive Book of Body Language* (2004, rev. 2017)**
- Two niches Navarro skips: gender-difference signals + cross-cultural inversions (palm gestures, head-nods).
- CZ as *Řeč těla* (Portál). EN audiobook on Audible (2025 Recorded Books).

**Vanessa Van Edwards — *Cues* (2022)**
- Modern, charisma-as-warmth-×-competence model. Translates Navarro's static catalogue into *active deployment* (what cues YOU send).
- EN only. Audiobook recommended (author narrates demos better).

### Tier 3 — Specialized

- **Navarro — *Three Minutes to Doomsday* / CZ *Tři minuty do soudného dne* (Grada 2017)** — narrative memoir, methodology in action.
- **Navarro — *Dangerous Personalities* / CZ *Nebezpečné osobnosti* (Grada 2015)** — narcissist/predator/paranoid/unstable taxonomy. Use for due-diligence.
- **Navarro — *Louder Than Words* / CZ *Jak prokouknout kolegy i obchodní partnery* (Grada 2011)** — workplace applications, mostly subsumed by *Be Exceptional*.

## Sub-skill 2: `tactical-empathy` (Voss verbal layer)

### Tier 1 — Required canon

**Chris Voss & Tahl Raz — *Never Split the Difference* (2016)**
- The only modern verbal-tactics book operationalizing FBI hostage primitives (mirroring, labeling, calibrated questions, late-night-FM-DJ voice, no-oriented questions, Black Swans, Ackerman bargaining).
- **CZ — *Nikdy nedělej kompromis* (Jan Melvil Publishing, 2016)**, transl. Petr Hanák.
- ⭐ **CZ audiobook on Audioteka, 9h 9min, 389/311 Kč klub. 4.9/5 (69 reviews) — top-rated CZ audiobook v korpusu.**
- EN audiobook gold standard: Storytel/Audible (Michael Kramer).

**Vecchi, Van Hasselt & Romano — Crisis (hostage) negotiation paper (2005)**
- Academic source for Behavioral Change Stairway Model (BCSM): Active Listening → Empathy → Rapport → Influence → Behavioral Change. **Voss is BCSM dressed in narrative.**
- Open PDF (gocit.org, SemanticScholar, nsuworks.nova.edu). ~18 pages. **Free.** Acquire immediately.

### Tier 2 — Strong supplements

**Mark Goulston — *Just Listen* (2009)**
- Psychiatrist's take on Voss-adjacent skills. Persuasion Cycle (Resisting → Listening → Considering → Willing → Doing → Glad I did). Stronger than Voss on de-escalating high-emotion contexts (firing, family-business, cofounder fights).
- EN only. Audible.

**George Kohlrieser — *Hostage at the Table* (2006, 2nd ed. 2024)**
- IMD Lausanne professor + practicing hostage negotiator. Translates hostage tactics into business/leadership. 7 keys to dialogue. The "self-regulation before tactics" prerequisite.
- EN only. Audiobook released 2024.

### Tier 3 — Specialized

**Gary Noesner — *Stalling for Time* (2010)**
- Founder of FBI Crisis Negotiation Unit (Voss's mentor). Memoir, not how-to. Origin story of FBI doctrine — Waco, Ruby Ridge.
- EN only. Audible (author narrates).

⭐ **Adam Dolník & Martin Moravec — *Svět elitního vyjednavače* (2024) — CZ-canonical**
- Czech equivalent to Noesner — Czech who has actually negotiated with hostage-takers, kidnappers, Somali pirates, ISIS. Trains FBI and Scotland Yard. **The single CZ-language extreme-stakes verbal tactics source.**
- ⭐ **CZ audiobook on Audioteka, 8h 37min, 399 Kč. 5.0/175 reviews — top-tier production.**

## Sub-skill 3: `batna-strategy` (Fisher/Ury content structure)

### Tier 1 — Required canon

**Roger Fisher, William Ury & Bruce Patton — *Getting to Yes* (1981, 3rd ed. 2011)**
- Founding text of principled negotiation. 4 pillars + BATNA = non-negotiable shared vocabulary.
- **CZ — *Jak dosáhnout souhlasu* (Management Press)**, older ed. *Dohoda jistá* (1994).
- EN audiobook on Audible/Storytel. **No CZ audiobook.**

**Deepak Malhotra & Max Bazerman — *Negotiation Genius* (2007)**
- Harvard Business School complementary masterpiece. ZOPA, anchor-shifting, multi-issue trades, bias exploitation/defense, contingency contracts.
- EN only. Audible (Fred Sanders, 11h).

### Tier 2 — Strong supplements

**Roger Fisher & Daniel Shapiro — *Beyond Reason* (2005)**
- Harvard Negotiation Project's own correction. 5 Core Concerns (appreciation, affiliation, autonomy, status, role) — interests beneath interests.
- EN only. Audible.

**Stone, Patton & Heen — *Difficult Conversations* (1999, 10th anniv. 2010)**
- Three-conversations frame (What Happened / Feelings / Identity). Best for *internal* prep before high-stakes talks (cofounder split, salary, firing).
- CZ ed. *Obtížné rozhovory* referenced (verify availability). EN audiobook (2024 re-recording on Audible).

### Tier 3 — Specialized

- **Lax & Sebenius — *3-D Negotiation* (2006)** — strategic-architectural setup framework for high-stakes multi-party (M&A, fundraising).
- **Malhotra — *Negotiating the Impossible* (2016)** — 3-strategy framework for deadlocked situations (Frame Control / Process / Empathy + Multi-issue).
- **William Ury — *Getting Past No* (1991)** — solo follow-up for hostile counterparts. 5-step "breakthrough" method.

## Sub-skill 4: `negotiation` orchestrator (flow + Cialdini bias + crisis variants)

### Tier 1 — Required canon

**Robert Cialdini — *Influence* (1984, "New & Expanded" 2021)**
- 7 principles (reciprocity, commitment/consistency, social proof, liking, authority, scarcity, unity). The bias layer.
- **CZ — *Nové zbraně vlivu* (2023)** — current ed. matching 2021 expansion.
- ⭐ **CZ audiobook on Audiolibrix.** EN audiobook on Audible (author narrates).

⭐ **Radim Pařík — *Umění vyjednat cokoliv* (2024, updated 2025) — CZ-canonical**
- Pařík completed FBI negotiation program + Harvard. Founded Asociace vyjednávačů. Adapts Voss/Navarro to CZ business reality.
- ⭐ **CZ audiobook on Audioteka, 11h, 389/311 Kč klub. 4.7/5 (12 reviews). Author narrates.** Grada Publishing.
- **The single most-important CZ-language synthesis.**

### Tier 2 — Strong supplements

**Robert Cialdini — *Pre-Suasion* (2016)**
- Privileged moments + channeled attention frame. Maps directly onto pre-meeting phase (room, opening question, opening anchor).
- **CZ — *Před-svědčování* (Jan Melvil Publishing). CZ audiobook on Audiolibrix.**

⭐ **Vít Prokůpek — *Psychologie ovlivňování* (orig. ~2014, 2nd ed.)**
- Practical CZ-specific cheat-sheet to Pařík's comprehensive treatment.
- ⭐ **CZ audiobook on Audioteka, 3h 49min, 379/303 Kč klub. Narrated by Vítek Martinec.** Listenable in 2 commutes.

**Robert Greene — *The Laws of Human Nature* (2018)**
- 18 laws covering deeper personality dynamics. Where Cialdini gives 7 levers, Greene gives 18 *people types*. Caveat: pop-history examples, not peer-reviewed — pattern library, not proof.
- EN only. Audible (28h, abridged version exists).

### Tier 3 — Specialized

- **Oren Klaff — *Pitch Anything* (2011) + *Flip the Script* (2019)** — pitcher-side framing. STRONG method, frame control. Use only when pitching (capital, SaaS to enterprise, agency retainers).
- **Jeb Blount — *INKED* (2020)** — sales-side closing/negotiation. 7 Immutable Rules + DEAL framework + ACED Buyer Persona.
- **Neil Rackham — *SPIN Selling* (1988)** — empirical-research bedrock of consultative selling. Sales-only.
- **William Ury — *Getting to Yes with Yourself* (2015)** — 7 steps to clarify what *you* truly want before sitting down.
- **Jiří Plamínek — *Konflikty a vyjednávání* (Grada 2012)** — older CZ corporate-trainer style. Optional CZ baseline.
- **Jaro Křivohlavý — *Konflikty mezi lidmi* (Portál 2002)** — Czech academic ancestor pre-FBI era. Skip unless writing in academic register.

## Deliberately excluded

- **Ekman *Telling Lies* / FACS** — superseded by Navarro's post-2018 position (microexpressions ≠ reliable deception detector)
- **Stuart Diamond *Getting More*** — methodologically scattered; subsumed by Malhotra/Bazerman
- **Jim Camp *Start with No*** — Voss's no-oriented questions cover the operational kernel
- **Herb Cohen *You Can Negotiate Anything*** — historical importance only
- **Kahneman *Thinking, Fast and Slow*** — Cialdini does negotiation-specific translation
- **NLP-flavored books** (Bandler, Grinder) — empirical support thin
