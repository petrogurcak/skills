# 50+ Proven Hook Formulas

Hooks organized by type and use case. Use these formulas to stop the scroll and open curiosity loops.

## Number-Based Hooks

### Direct Numbers
1. **[Specific $] saved. [Timeframe].**
   - "€6,300 saved. One machine."
   - "€500 wasted. Every month."

2. **[New price] vs [Old price]**
   - "€15,300 new. €9,000 rebuilt."
   - "€12,000 then. €6,000 now."

3. **[Number] + [Outcome]. [Zero/No] [Negative].**
   - "350+ cafés. Zero downtime."
   - "24 months. No breakdowns."

4. **[Large number] [units]. [Timeframe].**
   - "500+ shots. Daily. Zero issues."
   - "87 repairs. This month. 100% on time."

5. **[% Stat]. [Surprising fact].**
   - "50% savings. Same machine."
   - "41% less. Zero compromise."

### Timeline Numbers
6. **[Day]: [Event]. [Next day]: [Outcome].**
   - "Saturday: breakdown. Monday: running."
   - "2019: started. 2024: 350+ locations."

7. **[Time period] to [Result]**
   - "18 hours to Sweden."
   - "24 hours to perfect espresso."

8. **[Number] [units] in [timeframe]. [Result].**
   - "3 machines in 5 years. Still going strong."
   - "1,000+ repairs in 2024. Learn something new every time."

## Question Hooks

### Problem Questions
9. **Why does [common problem]?**
   - "Why does your espresso taste different every hour?"
   - "Why does your grinder waste so much coffee?"

10. **[Action] and still [problem]?**
    - "Servicing regularly and still breaking down?"
    - "Buying premium beans and still inconsistent shots?"

11. **What if [contrary to belief]?**
    - "What if your machine didn't lose value?"
    - "What if refurbished was better than new?"

12. **Need [outcome]?**
    - "Need it fixed today?"
    - "Need equipment that holds value?"

### Discovery Questions
13. **Ever wondered [mystery]?**
    - "Ever wondered why some machines last 20 years?"
    - "Ever wondered what happens to trade-ins?"

14. **Want to know [intriguing fact]?**
    - "Want to know why we buy back every machine?"
    - "Want to know the real cost of downtime?"

15. **How does [surprising thing]?**
    - "How does a 1987 Linea still work perfectly?"
    - "How does refurbished save you €6,300?"

### Choice Questions
16. **[Option A] or [Option B]?**
    - "€15,000 new or €9,000 rebuilt?"
    - "24-hour fix or 2-week wait?"

17. **Which would you choose: [scenario]?**
    - "Which would you choose: New machine, no warranty? Or rebuilt, 2-year warranty?"

## Before/After Hooks

### Temporal Before/After
18. **[Year]: [Before]. [Year]: [After].**
    - "2019: One machine. 2024: Still going strong."
    - "Monday: dead. Wednesday: perfect shots."

19. **[Before state]. Now [after state].**
    - "Used to replace yearly. Now decade-old machines."
    - "Waited 2 weeks for repairs. Now 24 hours max."

20. **From [problem] to [solution] in [timeframe]**
    - "From inconsistent to perfect in 3 days."
    - "From €15,000 to €9,000 in one decision."

### Transformation Hooks
21. **[Customer] had [problem]. [Solution]. Now [outcome].**
    - "Café had daily breakdowns. We rebuilt their machine. Now 2 years, zero issues."

22. **We used to [old way]. Then [realization]. Now [new way].**
    - "We used to sell new. Then realized waste. Now we rebuild."

## Controversial/Provocative Hooks

### Unpopular Opinion
23. **Unpopular opinion: [contrary statement]**
    - "Unpopular opinion: Buying new is wasting money."
    - "Unpopular opinion: Service matters more than brand."

24. **Everyone says [common belief]. [Contrary statement].**
    - "Everyone says newer is better. We've proved otherwise."
    - "Everyone fears refurbished. 350+ cafés don't."

25. **[Common assumption] is wrong.**
    - "Thinking machines lose value is wrong."
    - "Premium means expensive is wrong."

### Direct Challenge
26. **You're [doing something wrong]. Here's why.**
    - "You're wasting €500 monthly. Here's why."
    - "You're replacing too early. Here's the truth."

27. **Stop [common practice]. Do [better alternative].**
    - "Stop buying new. Start rebuilding."
    - "Stop waiting weeks. Get 24-hour service."

## Stat/Proof Hooks

### Achievement Stats
28. **[Number] [achievement]. [Proof point].**
    - "350+ locations. Same promise to each."
    - "24-hour guarantee. Never broken."

29. **[Impressive metric] and [another metric].**
    - "87 repairs last month and zero overtime."
    - "500+ shots daily and perfect every time."

30. **We've [done X]. We've learned [Y].**
    - "We've rebuilt 350 machines. We've learned what lasts."
    - "We've driven 50,000km this year. We've seen every problem."

### Comparison Stats
31. **[Metric]. [Timeframe]. [Comparison].**
    - "24-hour max downtime. 5 years. Never broken."
    - "€9,000 rebuilt. €15,300 new. No contest."

32. **[Industry norm] vs [our result]**
    - "Industry standard: 2-week repairs. Ours: 24 hours."
    - "Normal depreciation: 60%. Ours: Machines gain value."

## Hook Selection Guide

### For Instagram Posts:
- **High engagement:** Questions (#9, #11, #12)
- **Scroll-stop:** Numbers (#1, #2, #3)
- **Storytelling:** Before/After (#18, #19)
- **Brand building:** Story (#33, #34, #36)

### For Instagram Stories:
- **Quick impact:** Questions (#12, #16)
- **Visual:** Numbers (#7, #18)
- **Emotional:** Story (#36, #37)

### For Web Copy:
- **Headlines:** Benefits (#43, #44)
- **Subheads:** Problem/Solution (#46, #47)
- **CTAs:** Urgency (#51, #52)

### For Email Subject Lines:
- **Open rates:** Numbers (#1, #2, #10)
- **Curiosity:** Questions (#13, #14)
- **Urgency:** Time-limited (#51, #52)

## Advanced: Hook Stacking

Combine hook types for maximum impact:

**Number + Question:**
"€5,000 wasted annually. Want to know how?"

**Controversy + Proof:**
"Refurbished beats new. 350+ cafés agree."

**Problem + Before/After:**
"Inconsistent shots? 2019: same problem. 2024: solved."

**Shock + Story:**
"€20,000 machine. Let me show you why it's worth it."

## Hook Testing Framework

Test 3-5 variants for each piece:
1. Number hook
2. Question hook
3. Story hook
4. Controversy hook
5. Benefit hook

Track which performs best for your audience and double down on that type.

---

Remember: The hook's job is to stop the scroll and open a curiosity loop. The substance delivers value. The payoff closes the loop. All three must work together.
