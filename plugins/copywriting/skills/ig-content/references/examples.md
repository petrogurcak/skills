# Before/After Examples

This file contains concrete examples of copy transformations using Otto Bohuš principles.

## Instagram Posts

### Example 1: Product Sale

**❌ BEFORE (Generic, fluffy):**

```
We're excited to announce that we have a beautiful, high-quality La Marzocco Linea PB 3AV available for purchase! This amazing machine has been professionally restored by our experienced team and comes with a comprehensive warranty. It's a great opportunity for cafés looking for quality equipment at an affordable price. Contact us for more information!

#coffee #espresso #lamarzocco #cafe #quality #professional #coffeemachine
```

**Problems:**

- Too many generic adjectives ("beautiful", "high-quality", "amazing")
- Vague claims ("professionally restored", "affordable price")
- No specific numbers
- Too many hashtags
- Weak CTA
- No clear hook

**✅ AFTER (Otto + IG Framework):**

```
€15,300 new.
€9,000 rebuilt.

La Marzocco Linea PB 3AV
Fully refurbished.
Ready to work.

Save €6,300.
Get the same machine.

Available now.
DM us 👇

#lamarzocco #lineapb
```

**Improvements:**

- Concrete numbers (€15,300, €9,000, €6,300)
- Short, punchy sentences
- Clear value proposition
- Strong hook (price comparison)
- Removed generic words
- Single CTA
- Minimal hashtags

## Common Before/After Patterns

### Pattern 1: Replace Adjectives with Numbers

❌ "Many satisfied customers"
✅ "350+ locations"

❌ "Fast service"
✅ "24-hour maximum"

❌ "Affordable pricing"
✅ "50% less than new"

❌ "Experienced team"
✅ "Since 2019"

### Pattern 2: Convert Features to Benefits

❌ "Built-in scales"
✅ "Never waste coffee - perfect dose every time"

❌ "Dual boiler system"
✅ "Steam and brew simultaneously - zero wait time"

❌ "PID temperature control"
✅ "Same perfect shot. Every single time."

### Pattern 3: Shorten Sentences

❌ "We would like to inform you that we have recently completed the comprehensive restoration of a La Marzocco machine."
✅ "La Marzocco rebuilt. Ready to ship."

❌ "Our team of experienced technicians has been providing professional service for many years."
✅ "24-hour response. Zero downtime. Since 2019."

### Pattern 4: Add Triáda

❌ "We offer service and sales"
✅ "Buy back. Rebuild. Resell."

❌ "Fast and reliable"
✅ "Fast. Reliable. Guaranteed."

❌ "Quality machines and good prices"
✅ "Same machine. Half price. Zero compromise."

### Pattern 5: Story Over Statement

❌ "We provide fast service"
✅ "Saturday: machine died. Sunday: we fixed it. Monday: café opened."

❌ "Our machines are reliable"
✅ "350+ locations. 5 years. Zero downtime longer than 24 hours."

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **Numbers beat adjectives** - Every time
2. **Short beats long** - Always
3. **Benefits beat features** - No exceptions
4. **Stories beat facts** - More memorable
5. **Triáda beats lists** - Better comprehension
6. **Specific beats vague** - More believable
7. **One CTA beats many** - Higher conversion
8. **Question beats statement** - More engaging
9. **White space beats density** - Easier reading
10. **Direct beats polite** - Clearer action
