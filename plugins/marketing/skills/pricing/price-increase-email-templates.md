# Price Increase Email Templates

Based on Dima Melnik's methodology. Each template applies the 10-step framework for maximum customer retention.

---

## Template 1: Advance Notice Email (60-90 days before)

**Purpose:** First announcement, positive framing, loyalty discount offer

**Subject Line Options (PI-6 - Positive Framing):**
- "What's New at [Company] + Your Exclusive Reward"
- "Your Loyalty Reward Inside"
- "Exciting Updates for [Company] Members"

---

```
Subject: What's New at [Company] + Your Exclusive Reward

Hi [Name],

Thank you for being with us since [Year].

Over the past year, we've been busy improving [Product]:

• [Feature 1] - [Benefit in customer language]
• [Feature 2] - [Specific improvement, e.g., "40% faster"]
• [Feature 3] - [Outcome, e.g., "saves you 3 hours/week"]
• [Feature 4] - [New capability]
• [Feature 5] - [Enhancement]

(PI-7: List 5+ improvements)

Starting [Date + 60-90 days], we're adjusting our pricing to reflect these improvements and continue building the best [Product] possible.

**Your new price:** $[X]/month

**Your Loyalty Reward (PI-2):**
As a customer since [Year], you qualify for a [X]% loyalty discount:
- **Your special price:** $[Y]/month (locked for 12 months)

(PI-9: Social proof)
Join 2,300+ companies who trust [Product] for their [use case].

**Lock in your current price (PI-10):**
Prepay for 12 months before [Date] and keep your current rate of $[Z]/month.

[CTA: Lock in Current Price]

Questions? Reply to this email - I read every response.

[Signature]

P.S. The new pricing takes effect on [Date]. You have until [Date - 7 days] to decide.
```

## Template 2: Final Reminder Email (7 days before)

**Purpose:** Urgency, last chance for action

**Subject Line Options:**
- "7 Days Left: Your Price Decision"
- "Final Reminder: Lock in Your Rate"
- "[Name], Quick Decision Needed"

---

```
Subject: 7 Days Left: Lock in Your Current Rate

Hi [Name],

Quick reminder: your current [Product] rate of $[Current]/month is available for lock-in until [Date].

After [Date], your new rate will be $[New]/month.

**Your options:**

1. **Lock in current rate** - Prepay 12 months at $[Current]/month
   [CTA: Lock in Now]

2. **Accept new rate with loyalty discount** - $[Discounted]/month
   [CTA: Accept New Rate]

3. **Do nothing** - New rate applies automatically on [Date]

(PI-7: Brief reminder of value)
Remember: your [Product] subscription includes all the new features we've added this year, including [Top Feature].

Questions? Just reply.

[Signature]
```

## Customization Notes

### Placeholders to Replace:
- `[Company]` - Your company name
- `[Product]` - Your product name
- `[Name]` - Customer first name
- `[Year]` - Year they became customer
- `[Date]` - Implementation date
- `[X], [Y], [Z]` - Pricing numbers
- `[Feature 1-5]` - Your improvements
- `[Plan Name]` - Their plan tier

### Segment Variations:

**For long-term customers (3+ years):**
- Emphasize tenure: "As one of our founding members..."
- Higher loyalty discount (15-25%)
- Personal tone from CEO/founder

**For high-value customers:**
- Personal email from account manager
- Phone call option offered
- Custom retention offer if needed

**For at-risk customers (low engagement):**
- Lead with value reminder
- Offer downgrade option
- Re-engagement offer

### A/B Testing Suggestions:

**Subject lines to test:**
- Positive framing vs. direct ("Your Loyalty Reward" vs. "Important Update")
- Personalization ("[Name], something special for you")
- Urgency ("7 days left" vs. "Before [Date]")

**Content to test:**
- Number of improvements listed (5 vs. 3)
- Loyalty discount placement (early vs. late)
- CTA button text ("Lock in Now" vs. "Keep My Rate")

## Email Sequence Timeline

```
Day -90: Template 1 (Advance Notice)
Day -60: Reminder 1 (Abbreviated Template 1)
Day -30: Reminder 2 (Value-focused)
Day -7:  Template 2 (Final Reminder)
Day 0:   Template 3 (Implementation)
Day +7:  Check-in (Optional, for high-value customers)
```

**Between emails:** Monitor opens, clicks, replies. Adjust messaging based on response.

---

## Metrics to Track

| Metric | Target | Red Flag |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Email open rate | >50% | <30% |
| Click-through rate | >15% | <5% |
| Lock-in rate | >20% | <10% |
| Reply rate | >5% | N/A |
| Churn (30-day) | <5% | >10% |
| Support tickets | Normal | 3x+ increase |

**If red flags appear:** Pause migration, review messaging, consider additional value or extended timeline.
