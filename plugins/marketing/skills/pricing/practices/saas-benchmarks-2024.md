# SaaS Benchmarks 2024

**Source:** OpenView + High Alpha + Paddle
**Data:** 4,000+ companies, 800+ from 2024 survey
**Author:** Kyle Poyar (Growth Unhinged)

---

## Key 2024 Findings

### Early Stage SaaS is Thriving

| Metric | 2023 | 2024 | Change |
|--------|------|------|--------|
| Median headcount (early stage) | 12 | 7 | -42% |
| Top quartile growth (<$1M ARR) | 150% | 250% | +67% |
| AI company growth | Baseline | 2x higher | Significant |

**Insight:** Smaller teams building stronger businesses with lower CAC and better retention.

### Public SaaS Growth Slowdown

| Year | Median YoY Growth |
|------|-------------------|
| 2021 | 40% |
| 2022 | 35% |
| 2023 | 20% |
| 2024 | 15% |

## Net Revenue Retention (NRR) Benchmarks

### By Company Stage

| ARR | Bottom 25% | Median | Top 25% |
|-----|------------|--------|---------|
| <$1M | <90% | 100% | >115% |
| $1-5M | <95% | 105% | >120% |
| $5-20M | <100% | 110% | >125% |
| $20-50M | <105% | 115% | >130% |
| >$50M | <110% | 120% | >140% |

### By Go-to-Market Motion

| Motion | Median NRR |
|--------|------------|
| Product-Led Growth (PLG) | 105% |
| Sales-Led | 115% |
| Hybrid (PLG + Sales) | 120% |

**Insight:** Hybrid motion delivers best retention.

## Free Trial vs Freemium

### Conversion Rates

| Model | Median Conversion | Top Quartile |
|-------|-------------------|--------------|
| Free Trial (14 days) | 15% | >25% |
| Free Trial (30 days) | 12% | >20% |
| Freemium | 3% | >7% |
| Reverse Trial | 8% | >15% |

**Insight:** Shorter trials convert better. Freemium is acquisition, not revenue model.

---

## Pricing Page Benchmarks

### Number of Pricing Tiers

| # of Tiers | % of Companies | Conversion Impact |
|------------|----------------|-------------------|
| 2 tiers | 15% | Baseline |
| 3 tiers | 45% | +15% |
| 4 tiers | 30% | +10% |
| 5+ tiers | 10% | -20% |

**Optimal:** 3 tiers (Good-Better-Best)

### Annual vs Monthly Pricing

| Display Strategy | Impact |
|------------------|--------|
| Monthly only | Baseline |
| Annual only | -10% conversion, +20% LTV |
| Both (annual default) | +14% annual selection |
| Both (monthly default) | Baseline |

## Price Increase Data

### How Often Companies Raise Prices

| Frequency | % of Companies |
|-----------|----------------|
| Never | 25% |
| Every 2+ years | 30% |
| Annually | 35% |
| More than annually | 10% |

### Price Increase Impact

| Increase Size | Typical Churn Impact |
|---------------|---------------------|
| <10% | Minimal (<1% extra churn) |
| 10-20% | Low (1-3% extra churn) |
| 20-30% | Moderate (3-5% extra churn) |
| >30% | High (5-10%+ extra churn) |

**Best practice:** Annual increases of 5-15% with value communication.
